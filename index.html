Michelangelo Falco

I am a senior researcher linguist at the Leibniz-Zentrum Allgemeine Sprachwissenschaft (ZAS). Previously I was a post-doc at the Center for Mind/Brain Sciences of the University of Trento. My CV is available on ORCID.

I am interested in the distributional and referential properties of different noun phrases in Italian and other Romance languages. I use experimental methods and corpora to strengthen the empirical basis, and the toolbox of syntax and semantics for the analysis.

My recent research concerns Italian partitives and proportions. I am currently working on the distribution and referential properties of Italian words corresponding to English 'half'. My papers are available on LingBuzz. Datasets and code used for statistical analyses are shared on my GutHub
